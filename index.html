Poem For You

Like moonlight dancing on midnight sea,
Your love flows gently back to me.
A thousand miles can't dim the spark,
That burns for you inside my heart.

Your laugh, your soul, your gentle touch,
They’ve come to mean so very much.
In dreams, your fingers brush my skin
A love so pure, it lives within.

So here's my vow, both near and far,
No time or space can blur who you are.
Until I see you face to face,
You live in every warm embrace.
